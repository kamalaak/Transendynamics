THE COMPANY


Transen Dynamics was started in the year of 2018 with great passion for revolution in the field of robotics. Our products, concepts, designs are very unique . We design & develop from the simple sensor based device to the extra ordinary humanoid robots, automation tools for various industries. Offline based gps system , gas detectors for drainage systems and humanoid robot 'clara v1.0' are the few important inventions among those. Now, our company involves in 'clara v2.0' humanoid robot project followed by Mono electric vehicle.



FOUNDER

Kennithraj A, Founder & CEO of Transen Dynamics is a specialist in the field of Robotics and Artificial Intelligence. He has won several national and international awards for his achievements in the field of Robotics. He also gives technical and motivational talks in various TV channles, colleges, schools and writes technical articles for news papers. He always used to say, "What you think, you become".
